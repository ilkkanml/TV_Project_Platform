# 11 - Marketing Bible

This file defines the marketing positioning, messaging rules, public copy rules, landing page rules, pricing communication, reseller communication, customer communication, legal-safe language, and forbidden marketing claims for TV Project Platform.

Marketing must support the licensed player platform model.

Marketing must never make the product look like an IPTV provider, content provider, stream provider, channel seller, playlist provider, or broadcast backend.

## Product Boundary

TV Project Platform is a Licensed IPTV Player Platform.

The platform provides software/player access management.

The platform does not provide:

- TV channels
- Live streams
- VOD streams
- Channel packages
- Stream URLs
- IPTV playlists
- Playlist provider access
- Content catalogs
- Broadcast access
- CDN stream delivery

All marketing must respect this boundary.

## Core Marketing Position

Approved core position:

TV Project Platform is a licensed player platform for managing player access, subscriptions, device activation, reseller operations, payments, app versions, remote configuration, and app integration.

The platform is software.

The platform is not media.

The platform is not content.

The platform is not a broadcast provider.

## One-Sentence Description

Approved short description:

TV Project Platform helps users, resellers, and admins manage licensed player access, subscriptions, devices, payments, app versions, and remote configuration for supported TV devices.

## Extended Description

Approved extended description:

TV Project Platform is a licensed player access platform built for supported TV devices. It helps customers manage their software subscription and devices, helps resellers manage their own customers and credit, and helps admins control subscriptions, payments, app versions, remote config, and audit logs.

The platform does not provide channels, streams, playlists, or content. Users are responsible for using their own legal playlist or provider information.

## FAQ Approved Answers

Question:

Does TV Project Platform provide channels?

Approved answer:

No. TV Project Platform is a licensed player platform. It does not provide channels, streams, playlists, or content. Users are responsible for using their own legal playlist or provider information.

Question:

What am I paying for?

Approved answer:

Payments are for software/player access, subscription time, device activation rights, and platform features. Payments are not for channels, streams, playlists, or content.

Question:

Can I use my own playlist?

Approved answer:

The player app may allow users to enter their own legal playlist or provider information. Playlist credentials are stored locally on the device by default.
