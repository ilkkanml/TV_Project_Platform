# 11 - Marketing Bible

This file defines the marketing rules, positioning, copy direction, and public messaging for TV Project Platform.

Marketing must keep the product clearly positioned as a licensed player platform.

Marketing must not present the product as an IPTV provider, channel seller, stream provider, playlist provider, or content platform.

## Product Position

TV Project Platform is a Licensed IPTV Player Platform.

The product helps users manage:

- Player access
- Software subscriptions
- Device activation
- License status
- App access
- Reseller accounts
- Payment records
- App configuration

The product does not provide:

- TV channels
- Live streams
- VOD streams
- Channel packages
- Playlists
- Content catalogs
- Broadcast services

## Core Marketing Rule

Marketing must explain that the platform is software.

Marketing must not imply that channels, streams, playlists, or content are included.

Users are responsible for their own legal playlist or provider information.

## Approved Positioning

Approved positioning phrases:

- Licensed IPTV player platform
- Player license management platform
- Device activation platform
- Subscription management platform
- Reseller management platform

## Forbidden Positioning

Do not position the product as:

- IPTV provider

- Broadcast service

## Approved Short Description

TV Project Platform is a licensed player platform for managing software access, subscriptions, device activation, reseller operations, payments, app versions, and remote configuration.

## Approved Long Description

TV Project Platform is a licensed IPTV player platform designed to manage software access, customer subscriptions, device activation, reseller credit operations, payment records, app version rules, and remote app configuration.

The platform is not a content provider and does not provide channels, streams, playlists, or media content. Users are responsible for using their own legal playlist or provider information.

## FAQ Examples

Question:

Does TV Project Platform provide channels?

Answer:

No. TV Project Platform is a licensed player platform. It does not provide channels, streams, playlists, or content. Users are responsible for using their own legal playlist or provider information.

Question:

What am I paying for?

Answer:

Payments are for software access, player licensing, device activation, account features, or reseller credit. Payments are not for channels, streams, playlists, or content packages.

Question:

Where are playlist credentials stored?

Answer:

By default, playlist credentials are entered and stored locally inside the app using encrypted local storage. The backend is not the source of truth for playlist data.

Question:

Can I send a profile from the web panel to my device?

Answer:

Yes, an optional temporary encrypted transfer may be supported. The transfer is intended only for the user's own device and expires after use.
