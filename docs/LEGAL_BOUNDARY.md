# Legal Boundary

## Product Boundary

Nexora TV is a legal Core Media Player Ecosystem.

`TV_Project_Platform` is a platform/account/device/license/config/profile-transfer foundation.

It is not a content provider.

## Not Approved

The platform must not provide, enable, or imply:

- Content hosting
- Broadcasting
- Channel selling
- Bundled streams
- Stream relay
- Stream transcoding
- Provider credential sharing
- Unauthorized source extraction
- Rights-protection bypass or equivalent circumvention
- Illegal restreaming
- Claims of free access to paid channels or third-party content

## Allowed Direction

Allowed development/testing sources:

- Mock data
- Permitted test media
- Public demo media
- User-authorized legal sources
- Future licensed provider/API integrations with explicit approval

## Public Language Rule

Prefer:

- Legal Core Media Player Ecosystem
- User-authorized sources
- Bring your own authorized source
- Platform account/device/license/config center

Avoid public wording that implies:

- Paid channels are included
- The platform sells channels
- The platform provides free premium content
- The backend supplies stream lists

## Profile Transfer Boundary

Profile transfer may help a user move their own authorized profile/source data temporarily.

It must not become:

- Credential sharing
- Channel package transfer
- Provider scraping
- Permanent content storage
- Backend-controlled playlist source of truth

## Payment Boundary

Payment/subscription placeholders may exist for future planning.

Payment enforcement is inactive until a future Director-approved milestone.

## Implementation Rule

Any provider/API integration, public launch copy, reseller flow, or payment enforcement requires separate Legal Compliance review before implementation.
